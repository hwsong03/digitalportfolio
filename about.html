Born in South Korea and having lived in both Canada and the United States, I possess a multicultural perspective that enriches my work. Fluent in both Korean and English, I bridge cultural and linguistic gaps in my creative endeavors.

My primary interests lie in game design and visual graphics, where I have gained proficiency in a wide array of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GameMaker, Python, Java, and C++. Additionally, I am well-versed in Adobe Creative Suite applications, such as Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and Premiere, which I utilize to create impactful visual content.

I am committed to pushing the boundaries of design and technology, and I am excited to contribute my skills and creativity to innovative projects that inspire and engage users.
